Project Profile

Concrete Batch Plant – Complete Automation Upgrade

Turnkey upgrade of a dry-mix concrete batching plant, covering MCC redesign, full PLC/HMI program, field I/O, and commissioning – with minimal production downtime.

Location

Jordan

Client

Local concrete producer

Role

Lead Automation & Electrical Engineer

Status

Completed & in operation

Overview

What the plant does and why this project was needed.

The plant is a dry-mix concrete batching facility with multiple aggregate conveyors, weighing belts, cement screws, vibrators, water and admixture dosing, and a mixer. The existing control system was outdated, difficult to maintain, and caused frequent stoppages.

The goal was to deliver a modern, reliable control system with clear diagnostics, flexible recipe handling, and improved safety – while reusing as much of the existing power infrastructure as practical.

Objectives

  • Upgrade PLC/HMI and plant wiring
  • Implement reliable batching/recipe logic
  • Reduce troubleshooting time

Constraints

  • Limited shutdown window
  • Reuse of some existing motors & cables
  • Keep operation intuitive for existing operators

My Role

End-to-end responsibility from concept to commissioning.

  • Defined overall control philosophy & sequences
  • Sized and selected MCC components & protection
  • Designed EPLAN schematics and panel layouts
  • Prepared I/O list, cable schedule, and terminal plans
  • Developed PLC program and HMI screens
  • Commissioned drives, interlocks, and safety circuits
  • Performed testing, tuning, and operator training
  • Delivered as-built documentation & reports

Technical Stack

Main platforms, tools, and hardware used in this project.

Control & Software

  • Siemens TIA Portal – PLC & HMI
  • Structured control blocks for batching sequences
  • Alarm, event, and recipe management logic

Electrical & Panels

  • EPLAN Electric P8 – schematics & reports
  • EPLAN Pro Panel – 3D MCC layout
  • LV MCC with motor starters, VFDs, overloads

Field Devices

  • Load cells for cement & aggregate weighing
  • Level, speed, and position sensors
  • Vibrators, screw conveyors, pumps, mixer drive

Documentation

  • I/O list, cable list, terminal diagrams
  • SLD excerpts and load calculations
  • Functional descriptions & operator guide

Results

Key outcomes achieved after commissioning.

Operational

  • More stable batching cycles and reduced rework
  • Faster fault finding via clear alarms & diagnostics
  • Operators can change recipes safely and quickly

Engineering & Maintenance

  • Clean, consistent EPLAN documentation
  • Easier future expansions & modifications
  • Reduced unplanned downtime compared to old system
