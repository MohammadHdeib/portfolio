Process Plant

Emulsion Paints Plant Automation

Complete industrial automation design for a fully automated emulsion paint plant.
Process plant Complete automation design EPLAN & AutoCAD

Client: Al Quds Paints Industry

Location: Amman, Jordan

Industry: Chemicals / Paints

Date: December 2024

Responsible for the complete design and engineering of the industrial automation system for a fully automated emulsion paint plant. This included:

  • Electrical control panel design: layouts, schematics, power distribution, and component selection.
  • Remote I/O architecture and cabinet layout to optimize wiring and diagnostics.
  • System topology: planning the network structure for field devices, control panels, and supervisory systems.
  • Industrial communication: integration of PROFINET and other protocols (e.g. EtherCAT, Modbus).
  • I/O addressing and mapping for PLCs, remote stations, and field instruments.
  • Component specification for PLCs, VFDs, HMIs, power supplies, relays, and terminal blocks.
  • Design prepared for clean commissioning, troubleshooting, and future expansion.

Worked closely with cross-functional teams (software, commissioning, project management) to deliver a robust, scalable automation solution.

Cement / VRM

Cemonite Vertical Roller Mill

P&ID and VFD panel engineering for an eco-concrete vertical roller mill line.
P&ID & process design VFD control panels Manufacturing data

Client: Cemonite

Location: Sola, Norway

Industry: Cement / Eco-concrete

Date: June 2023 – December 2023

Supported the development of a pioneering vertical roller mill line for recyclable concrete production by designing detailed P&ID diagrams and electrical documentation.

  • P&ID design capturing process equipment, instrumentation, valves, sensors, and control loops.
  • Coordinated with mechanical, civil, and process teams to keep electrical design aligned with site works.
  • VFD control panel design: layouts and schematics for multiple drives and auxiliaries.
  • Generated manufacturing-ready data: drilling patterns, terminal plans, and bill of materials.
  • Ensured compliance with process requirements and industrial standards.

The project contributed to Cemonite’s green innovation goals by enabling automated production of eco-friendly building materials.

Feasibility Field Test – Advanced Exploration

EPLAN and 3D Pro Panel design for an advanced exploration field testing system.
Field test system EPLAN 2022 3D Pro Panel

Client: Team Power Solutions

Location: Saskatchewan, Canada

Industry: Engineering Solutions / Exploration

Date: June 2022

Contributed to the electrical design of a field testing project, supporting advanced exploration systems.

  • Developed electrical schematics based on project P&ID and Single Line Diagram (SLD).
  • Created EPLAN 2022 schematics aligned with process and power requirements.
  • Prepared custom terminal and connection reports to streamline wiring and field work.
  • Modeled the electrical control panel in 3D using EPLAN Pro Panel.
  • Coordinated with the engineering manager to match documentation with field and internal standards.

The focus was on producing manufacturing-ready designs that connected engineering intent with practical, on-site execution.
