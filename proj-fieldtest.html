Feasibility Field Test – Advanced Exploration

EPLAN and 3D Pro Panel design for an advanced exploration field testing system.
Field test system EPLAN 2022 3D Pro Panel

Client: Team Power Solutions

Location: Saskatchewan, Canada

Industry: Engineering Solutions / Exploration

Date: June 2022

For this feasibility field test project, I supported the electrical design of a system used for advanced exploration activities. The focus was on producing clear EPLAN documentation and a 3D panel model that translated engineering intent into something practical for construction and field work.

Key tasks and responsibilities:

  • Developed electrical schematics based on project P&ID and Single Line Diagram (SLD) provided by the engineering team.
  • Created EPLAN 2022 schematics aligned with process and power requirements, including detailed device and connection data.
  • Prepared custom terminal and connection reports to streamline panel wiring and field terminations.
  • Modeled the electrical control panel in 3D using EPLAN Pro Panel to validate space, routing, and assembly.
  • Coordinated with the engineering manager to ensure documentation matched internal standards and field realities.

The deliverables were aimed at reducing uncertainty in the field, ensuring that installers and technicians had a clear, consistent reference during build and commissioning.
