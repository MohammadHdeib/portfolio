Cemonite Vertical Roller Mill

Client: Cemonite
Location: Sola, Norway
Industry: Chemicals
Date: June, 2023 – Dec, 2023

This project supported Cemonite’s development of a vertical roller mill line for recyclable concrete production. I contributed to the engineering of the process and electrical documentation for a greenfield facility.

Key responsibilities:

  • P&ID design for equipment, instrumentation, valves, and control loops.
  • Coordination with mechanical, civil, and process teams for layout and site constraints.
  • VFD control panel engineering, including layouts, schematics, and drive integration.
  • Preparation of manufacturing-ready documentation: drilling patterns, terminals, BOM.
  • Ensuring compliance with relevant standards and safety requirements.
