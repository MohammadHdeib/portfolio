Cement / VRM

Cemonite Vertical Roller Mill

P&ID and VFD panel engineering for an eco-concrete vertical roller mill production line.
P&ID & process design VFD control panels Manufacturing-ready data

Client: Cemonite

Location: Sola, Norway

Industry: Cement / Eco-concrete

Date: June 2023 – December 2023

This project supported Cemonite’s development of a vertical roller mill line for recyclable concrete production. I contributed to the engineering of the process and electrical documentation for a new greenfield facility.

Key responsibilities:

  • P&ID design capturing process equipment, instrumentation, valves, sensors, and control loops associated with the vertical roller mill and auxiliaries.
  • Coordination with mechanical, civil, and process engineering teams to keep electrical design aligned with layout changes and site constraints.
  • VFD control panel engineering, including layouts, schematics, and integration of multiple drives and auxiliaries.
  • Preparation of manufacturing-ready documentation: drilling patterns, terminal diagrams, and bill of materials for panel builders.
  • Attention to standards and safety requirements relevant to cement and industrial plants in Norway.

The documentation was structured so that panel builders, installers, and commissioning teams could execute their work with minimal ambiguity, reducing delays and rework during construction.
